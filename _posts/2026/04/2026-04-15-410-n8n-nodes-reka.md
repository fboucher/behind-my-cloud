## Summary

We continued building the Reka AI connector for N8N by adding brand-new vision operations: prompt about an image, prompt about a short video, and detect objects in an image, powered by the Reka Edge model. Plenty of debugging along the way as we figured out how to properly structure the API calls and route between the different media types inside the connector!
