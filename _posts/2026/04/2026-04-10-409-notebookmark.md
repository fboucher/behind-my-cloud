## Summary

This stream was dedicated to NotebookMark, the .NET MAUI Android app I'm building to read articles and take notes offline on my e-reader — but we barely touched the code because the entire session turned into an Android dev environment battle on Linux. Between Android Studio behaving strangely on a multi-screen setup, hunting down the right SDK version, setting up an emulator, and finally fixing missing environment variables, the mini PC was pushed to its limits (and almost gave up on us!). By the end we got closer to a working setup, but next time I'll switch to a more powerful machine to finally review the Copilot-generated PR and actually write some MAUI code.
