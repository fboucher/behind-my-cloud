## Summary

On this Friday stream, we jumped back into the 2D6 Dungeon App to tackle a bunch of missing character sheet features, including XP tracking, gold and loot management, soak and bloody status, and god favor notes, all the little things you discover are missing when you actually play the game! We also took a fun detour to check out the brand new GitKraken 12.0 release with its shiny AI agent session view and work tree management features.
